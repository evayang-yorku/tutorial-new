Indigenous Friends App

@ York University

The Indigenous Friends App is a mobile application that seeks to create a safe space for Indigenous Youth (First Nations, Métis and Inuit) in Canada. It has three main objectives:

  • Create Indigenous Networks between Indigenous Youth.
  • Access to Traditional Indigenous Counseling.
  • Access to information about the available resources in the community.

    • If you are interested to enroll your institution, please contact: info@indigenousfriends.org

    Roles

    Eagles/Yellow:

    Elders who are mentors in charge of healing the Emotional and Physical Dimensions.

    Owl/Red:

    Traditional Keepers, Elders, or Indigenous Faculty Members who are in charge of healing the Intellectual and Spiritual Dimensions.

    Wolf/Black:

    Traditional Keepers or Staff Members who keep the community safe by managing the app policies and members' access.

    Bear/Blue:

    Experienced Students, Alumni, and Grad Students who have gained valuable knowledge throughout a long path of learning experiences.

    Turtle/Green:

    New users who support this community while walking and swimming to find their own life path.

    Marten/White:

    Non-Indigenous allied members who are adopted into our community to provide us with support and to help maintain this space.

    Beaver/Orange:

    Application developers who help build this space.

    We have created different roles for everyone. You can go to your Profile to change between the Animal Clan System or the Colour System.
